Expenses

Use these tips when submitting expenses after Converge.

Here is what you need to know to get reimbursed for any additional expenses that you incurred to travel to San Diego.

Remember that your round-trip airfare to San Diego, hotel accommodations at the Paradise Point Resort & Spa and the SuperShuttle between the San Diego Airport and hotel were billed directly by MD&I so you will not need to expense these items.

Allowable Expenses

  • Fees to obtain a U.S. Visa. This excludes Passport application or renewal fees.
  • Ground transportation on the days you departed and returned between your home and the airport. This does not include ground transportation in San Diego. If you did not use the SuperShuttle, you are responsible for the cost. This includes a car service/taxi, Lyft or Uber, or a rental car.
  • Baggage fees not included with your airfare.
  • Tips/gratuities you may have given on travel days.
  • Employee meal per diem on the dates you were on IBM business and when meals were not provided as part of Converge 2019.

The above expenses must follow the guidelines within the Travel@IBM Expense Reimbursement Policy. Refer to your country's expense reimbursement guidelines on the Country Specific Travel & Expense Resources on Travel@IBM.
