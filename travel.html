Booking Deadline

If you don't need a new or updated travel visa, please book travel by November 15, 2018. If you require a new or updated U.S. Visa to travel, you have until November 26, 2018 to make travel arrangements.

  • San Diego is on U.S. Pacific Time (PT) or UTC-8 hours.
  • The event will last 3 days— January 29, 30 and 31.  It begins at 7:30 a.m., PT, on January 29 and ends on January 31, at 3 p.m., PT.

Arrival: Monday, January 28, 2019, anytime

Booking your Stay

MD&I covers the 3 nights of your stay during the event and will pay the hotel directly. If you're unable to reserve a departure flight for Thursday, January 31st, MD&I will cover Thursday night, too.

To book, modify or cancel your stay, please visit this custom reservation page, created especially for MD&Iers. The hotel requires that you provide a credit card to book your reservation. This can be either your AMEX corporate credit card or a personal credit card.

You may also book reservations by phone. Call +1 (855) 463-3361 and select Option 5 to book a new reservation. You'll need to provide our group name, Converge. If you need to modify or cancel an existing reservation, please select Option 4.

Please note: the hotel's cancellation policy is 48 hours before your check-in date. If you cancel within 48 hours of your arrival date, your credit card will be charged for 1 night's stay (room rate and taxes). You're responsible for any cancellation fees.

Additional nights and guests

  • If you'd like to extend your stay in San Diego, the hotel is offering a discounted group rate of $210/night (excluding taxes and fees) at your expense. The discounted rate applies to personal stays on the 3 days before and 3 days after the event. Availability is a first-come, first-served basis.
  • If you're traveling with family or friends, you're responsible for their expenses, including any additional room occupancy charges.
  • We recommend that you confirm overnight accommodations before booking your flight.

    • Check-in

    Check-in time is 4 p.m., PT.  If you plan on arriving to the hotel earlier, please make a request directly with the hotel. If your room is not ready at the time of your check-in, the hotel will happily store your luggage for you.

    When checking in, you will need to present a credit card to pay for any incidentals, additional nights, occupancy charges if you have guests staying with you or any other personal fees you incur. These additional charges will not be reimbursed.

    Check-out

    Check-out time is 11 a.m. If you're leaving on Thursday, remember to check-out before you head to the conference space that morning. There is no time in the schedule for you to go back to your room. Please store your bags in the Paradise Ballroom.

    You can speed up your check-out by using the ALICE app that was in your hotel confirmation email. Access the app and go under the “Front Desk” option and choose "Check Out”. NOTE: If you’ve incurred any additional charges (incidentals, additional nights, additional occupancy charges) it would be best if you clear those at the front desk as they are your responsibility and will not be reimbursed.

International Travelers

Please make sure you:

  • Have a passport — if your passport is expired, it is your responsibility to renew it at your own expense.
  • Have a valid travel visa — if you require a U.S. Visa, work with your Travel Lead. If your country requires you to travel to a U.S. Embassy/Consulate and/or submit a letter from the IBM event host, please contact your local Travel Lead. If you already have a U.S. Visa from a previous trip, check that it is not expired.
  • Have a valid AMEX corporate card – application available on Travel@IBM
  • Look for an email from your local travel lead with additional instructions.

      • Personal side trips

      Your airfare must be the lowest logical round-trip for our event's travel dates and between your home departure city and San Diego (adhering to the round-trip airfare caps listed above). You may only add a personal side trip to your reservation if the additional flight stays within that lowest logical round-trip fare. If adding a side trip would incur IBM additional costs (even if its under the fare cap), airline reservations and costs for flights for a personal side trip are your responsibility.

MD&I has prepaid for a shuttle service for transportation between the San Diego International Airport and Paradise Point Resort & Spa. This prepaid service is for MD&I employees only.

Any fees for car rentals, car service, taxis, or rideshares like Uber and Lyft will be at your cost.
